Curry

Description

Curry is a commonly found dish in the country of India. It is remarked by its bold flavors and its thick creamy cosistency. Its abundance of spice that attack the tongue is at the forefront of its signature flavor profile. It has inpsired variants in other countries such as Japan and Thailand.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 can of coconut cream

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Saute onion until browned.
  3. Stir in garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, sugar and salt.
  4. Continue stirring for 2 minutes.
  5. Add chicken pieces, tomato sauce, yogurt, and coconut milk.
  6. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
